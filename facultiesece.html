Electronics and Communication Engineering

Course Description & Detail

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering at RVSCET has a top-level quality assessment rating for teaching, and reputation for research. It was established in the year 2004 with the avowed intent of raising highly qualified Engineers and Researchers who can make substantial contribution to the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The department is guided by a team of devoted and diligent faculty and staff members. The department has well equipped laboratories such as Basic Electronics, Analog and Digital Electronics, Solid State Device, Analog, Digital, Optical, Advance Communication, Microwave , RF Communication System, VLSI Design, Signal System, DSP, Electronics Design, Microprocessor, Microcontroller etc. Advance software’s like HFSS, Multisim, MATLAB, VISSIM-COMM etc. are the pride of the department. The Electronics and Communication department conducts regular Co-curricular activities like Workshop, Seminars, Invited talks by renowned personalities from industries and academic institutions.

The inputs from visiting professors and advisers from industries are used for continuous quality improvement of the students in order to meet the needs of the industries, which are among the reasons why students all over the country chose to pursue their study in Electronics and Communication Engineering at this department. In every year 75 – 80% students are placed in different reputed industries, 15 -20% students goes for higher study in India and abroad and about 1-5% become entrepreneur.
