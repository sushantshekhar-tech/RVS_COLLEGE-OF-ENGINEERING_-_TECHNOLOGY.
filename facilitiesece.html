The department provides computing facilities with separate computers to each student. All laboratories are connected to 20Mbps internet connection. This laboratory is specially designed for Electronics & Communication engineering department to facilitate the students to do their mini and major projects inside the campus. It has 30 personal computers. All are networked and well furnished. Projects vary from different software and application software development, design and optimization of new algorithms, audio/video compression software, IoT, Image processing, Machine Learning and network related projects.



In addition to the Central Library Departments have their own Library. This contains important titles and Journals in the respective branches of study. Students and Staff are advised to fully utilize these books, previous year project thesis, Gate materials and Journals for further enhancement of their subject knowledge books and Journals will not be issued any one, but are available only for reference wish in the Departmental HOD and faculty In-charge controls the functionality of department Library.
