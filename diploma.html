Diploma in Civil Engineering

Course Description & Detail

Civil Engineering is a highly respected field that has played a crucial role in society’s development and progress. Through this degree program, students gain the knowledge and skills necessary to design and manage a wide range of infrastructure projects, from buildings and water systems to transportation networks. Graduates are in high demand across a variety of industries and have the potential to pursue advanced research and study in this exciting and rewarding field.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Course Description & Detail

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at the college has been operational since its inception in 2004 with an intake capacity of 120 students. Their vision is to produce excellent Mechanical Engineers who can excel in different functional areas in industries and academia for the betterment of society. They provide quality education, a disciplined learning environment, well-equipped workshops and laboratories, and hands-on experience with conventional and non-conventional projects. The faculty members are highly qualified and experienced with academic and industrial backgrounds.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering

Course Description & Detail

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department aims to produce skilled engineers with a focus on practical knowledge. The faculty comprises experienced members with M. Tech degrees in various specializations. The department has a strong academic record with top rank holders in Ranchi University and Kolhan University among its alumni.
