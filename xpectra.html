Introducing Xpectra - The Media Club of RVSCET!

Xpectra is a vibrant community of creative enthusiasts at RVSCET who are passionate about all things media. Our club serves as a platform for students to explore their talents, collaborate, and express themselves through various forms of media. Through Xpectra, we aim to bring together budding photographers, filmmakers, writers, designers, and artists, fostering a dynamic environment where ideas flourish and skills are honed. Whether you have a knack for capturing breathtaking visuals, crafting compelling stories, or designing eye-catching graphics, Xpectra welcomes all media enthusiasts with open arms. Our club organizes a range of engaging activities and workshops throughout the year. From photography expeditions and film screenings to writing competitions and graphic design tutorials, we offer diverse opportunities for members to learn, grow, and showcase their talents. Xpectra believes in the power of collaboration and teamwork. We encourage our members to work together on projects, pooling their skills and expertise to create impactful media content. We also provide a platform for networking with professionals in the media industry, offering valuable insights and guidance to help students navigate their chosen paths. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn the ropes or an experienced media aficionado looking to expand your horizons, Xpectra has something to offer you. Join us, and together, let's unleash the full potential of media and creativity at RVSCET!

Prof. Abhilash Ghosh (Head of Xpectra)

The Xpectra Team is guided by prof Abhilash Ghosh of Electrical and Electronics branch.
